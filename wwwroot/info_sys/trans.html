What are the Evolving business usage of computers ?
  • computers crunch numbers
  • Book-keeping & Accounts
  • Find,organize and display data:
    • low power main frame system
  • Model system and predict:
    • High power main frame systems
  • Interact with decision makers:
    • Corporate network & VDU on a desk
  • Interact with customers:
    • Internet & PC at home
What are the organizational changes?
  • Isolation: cost reduction in accounting
  • Consolidation: lots of applications BUT poor documentation and control
  • The Freeze: Restrained by controls and stanfards
  • User Demand: Direct access, enquiry systems
  • Free for all: PC,easy to use packages
What are the management functions?
  • Planning:
    • Setting goals,developing policies.
  • Organising:
    • establishing structure, assigning tasks
  • Resource allocation:
    • labour,materials
  • Directing:
    • coordination,delegation
  • controlling:
    • monitoring and correcting
What are the levels of management ?
  • Top level management
  • Middle level management
  • Low level management
What are the characteristics of Decision making ?
  • Intelligence:
    • Recognise problem Search for information
  • Design:
    • Develop and evaluate alternative courses of action
  • Choice:
    • Select the course to be followed
What are Transaction processing systems ?
  • Functional:
    • Directly supports business functions Day-to-day activity
  • Deterministic:
    • Always has a defined outcome
  • Routine:
    • Mechanistic – rule based
    • Low level of decision making
