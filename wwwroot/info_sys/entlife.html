What are events?
  • What are events ?
  • Something that happens or changes in the environment
  • A point in time
  • May be start or end of an activity
  • Need a response from the “system” OR
  • Need to be recorded by the “system” OR
  • Both
What is the state/status of an object ?
  • A recognisable period in its life history
  • In general different states imply:
    • Different capabilities
    • Different constraints
    • Different characteristics
  • Events change states
  • Used to represent the states (in the life) of a Data Entity
  • Also used to describe all aspects of system behaviour and sequences of events:
    • From computer programs and
    • user interface dialogues
    • to business processes
    • State models encode business logic
    • State models can get very complicated
    • State models should focus on the important or significant changes
      • Next normal event is different
      • Allowable changes are very different
