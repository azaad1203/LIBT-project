About Us

This website will provide students with information for the modules that they will be studying as well as some exercises to test their knowledge on what they have read and learnt, upon completing a certain module or a topic. This website is useful for numerous reasons.

Firstly, this website is available for every individual and can be accessed worldwide. The content provided in the website is completely free.

Secondly, the key concepts that the students may find difficult to comprehend are simplified, in an understandable format. Information is selected from various core textbooks therefore students will not need to purchase them.

Thirdly, many students use the Internet as one of their source to obtain some assistance for a certain module. Therefore this website would be useful as it can be accessed worldwide and be available at any time. This means the students can access the website at their convenience.

Lastly, with good understandable content, it can increase the knowledge of the student and could possibly increase their confidence for that particular subject.
