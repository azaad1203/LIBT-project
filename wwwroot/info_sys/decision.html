What is Management Information System (M.I.S)?
  • Takes data from transaction processing systems:
    • Accurate,Timely,Complete
    • produces summaries and statistics
    • (Relevant, Complete)
  • MIS
    • Monitor … Feedback … Control
    • Standard reports and statistics
    • Produced:
      • on a regular schedule
      • only exceptions
      • on demand
    • Include external information sources
      • e.g. sales of competitors
What is a Decision support system ?
  • An interactive system that helps managers come to a decision
    • Answers "What if...." questions
    • May take data from TPS or MIS
    • May use models or simulations
What is the concept of Data and Information ?
  • Data-raw facts,no specific use
  • Information-Processsed data for specific use
  • Characteristics of information:
    • Brief, Appropriate and Precise
      • Relevant
        • The data which is needed
        • specific to the task
      • Concise
        • Brief,no more than necessary
        • Without excessive detail
      • Complete
        • All relevant data
        • timely
        • accurate
