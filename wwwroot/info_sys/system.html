Key Concept

Systems

What are systems ?
  • A collection of parts or components
  • They have a defined structure or pattern connections betweeen the parts.
  • There may be interaction with the things outside the system
  • There are two types of systems:
    • open system:exchanges information with the environment
    • closed system: does not exchange information with the environment
How will you know it is a system ?
  • collection of components is not random
  • useful to talk about
  • Appear to belong together
  • Seem to be coherent
  • Exhibits goal seeking or purposive behaviour
  • systems can be divided into different components called subsystems
What is an Organized system ?
  • An independent system that has internal structures to maintain its purpose despite its environment.
  • The flows within the system that make it independent are information flows.
  • Information Systems are the sub-systems that enable us to manage (or control) an organisation (or human activity)
what is an Algorithm ?
  • An algorithm describes a process.
  • A sequence of instructions which inevitably lead to the solution of a problem.
  • Following the instructions does not require any “understanding” of the problem.
  • An algorithm written in a language for a computer it is a “program”.
what are information systems ?
  • Definition A (computer focused):A collection of interacting computer programs that supply Information to human Decision Makers.
  • Definition B (human focused):A collection of interacting computer(s) and humans that makes management decisions within an organisation
